FAQs

  • How do I win? Align your letters to form valid words — vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
  • What if a player is inactive? After 1 minute of no action, a countdown starts. If they still don’t move, you win!
  • Is internet required? No! You can play locally or against AI without internet.
